This is an AI-powered investment memo and competitive intelligence framework for the Agentic Supply Chain sector.

# 🛡️ Project Argus: AI-Driven Investment Intelligence 

### 📊 Scenario
A Tier-1 Venture Capital firm (Nexus Ventures) requires a high-stakes market analysis of the **Agentic Supply Chain** sector to decide on a $15M Series A investment.

### 🛠️ The Tech Stack
* **Perplexity Pro (Deep Research Mode):** Used to crawl 25+ real-time sources, verify funding data, and identify customer pain points in legacy ERP systems.
* **Gemini 1.5 Pro (Google AI Studio):** Used to synthesize a 120k+ token context window, cross-referencing market data to identify "Blue Ocean" opportunities.

### 📁 Project Structure
* **/output**: The final 5-page Investment Memo (PDF).
* **/prompts**: My "Chain of Thought" logic and multi-step prompting framework.
* **/research**: Source verification logs and Perplexity research links.

### 🚀 Key Insight
By leveraging a "Skeptical Agent" framework in Gemini, I identified three major regulatory hurdles that traditional AI summaries missed, saving the firm from high-risk exposure.
